HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
No dependencies.

01

Band detects water.

Each crew member wears a cheap band. Two contacts complete a circuit the instant they hit the sea.

02

Base confirms separation.

Wet band plus a dropping signal means someone is in the water and moving away. Two conditions — so a splash does not cry wolf.

03

Boat and shore are told.

Alarm sounds. The spot is marked on GPS. A message goes to a contact onshore over cellular or satellite.
